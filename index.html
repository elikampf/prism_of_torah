About Rabbi Prisman

Based in Ramat Beit Shemesh, Rabbi Asaf Aharon Prisman seamlessly blends traditional Torah scholarship with contemporary relevance. A distinguished alumnus of Yeshivat Or Chaim (Toronto) with advanced studies at Yeshivat Birkat Moshe and Netivot Olam, he currently learns at Kollel Beis Hillel while delivering daily shiurim. His acclaimed "Prism of Torah" podcast inspires audiences across six continents.
